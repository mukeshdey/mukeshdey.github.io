Mukesh Kumar Dey

PhD Scholar @ IIT Bombay

Hi, I'm Mukesh Kumar Dey, a PhD scholar at IIT Bombay. My research lies at the intersection of traditional hydrological modeling and advanced data-driven approaches. I am particularly interested in using advanced techniques to enhance the accuracy and reliability of hydrological models.

Currently, my focus is on integrating data assimilation techniques within a socio-hydrological model, applied to a large reservoir command area. This work aims to improve our understanding and prediction capabilities by blending both ground-based and satellite-based data sources. My approach not only enriches the model's performance but also provides deeper insights into complex water resource systems. Through my research, I hope to contribute toward more sustainable and effective water management practices in rapidly changing environments.
